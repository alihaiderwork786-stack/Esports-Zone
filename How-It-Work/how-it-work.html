Detailed Step-by-Step Guide

01

Account Registration

Create Your Player Profile

Start your esports journey by creating your account on Esports Zone.

What You Need:
  • Valid email address
  • Game username/nickname
  • Mobile number (for verification)
  • Preferred games you play
Registration Process:
  1. Click "Register" button on top right
  2. Fill in your basic information
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your player profile
  5. You're ready to join tournaments!

Account verified and ready

02

Buying Coins

Purchase Coins for Tournament Entries

All tournaments require coins for entry. Buy coins easily through our secure payment system.

Coins Can Be Used For:
  • Tournament entry fees
  • In-game purchases
  • Premium features
  • Special events access

Secure coin purchase

03

Joining Tournaments

Find and Register for Events

Browse through various tournaments and choose the ones that match your skills.

Tournament Types Available:
Paid Tournaments Entry fee required
Squad Battles Team competitions
Pro Leagues Professional level
Premium Events High stake tournaments
How to Join:
  1. Go to "Tournaments" page
  2. Filter by game, date, or type
  3. Click on tournament to view details
  4. Click "Join Tournament" button
  5. Pay entry fee using your coins
  6. Receive confirmation email

Tournament schedule confirmed

04

Competition & Matches

Play Your Matches

Compete against other players following our fair play rules and tournament structure.

Match Process:
1
Bracket Assignment

Get placed in tournament bracket automatically

2
Schedule Match

Coordinate match time with your opponent

3
Play Match

Play at scheduled time with fair play

4
Report Results

Submit match results with screenshots

Important Rules:
  • Be punctual for scheduled matches
  • No cheating or third-party tools
  • Respect other players
  • Submit valid proof of results
  • Follow game-specific rules

Fair competition ensured

05

Winning & Claiming Rewards

Receive Your Prizes

Claim your hard-earned rewards quickly and securely through our system.

Types of Rewards:
Cash Prizes Direct bank transfer
Esports Coins Platform currency
Gaming Gear Physical products
Premium Badges Profile recognition
How to Claim Rewards:
  1. Check tournament results page
  2. Go to "My Rewards" section
  3. Select the tournament you won
  4. Click "Claim Reward" button
  5. Provide payout details
  6. Receive confirmation
  7. Get your prize within 24-48 hours

Quick reward processing
